About Me

Go forth to serve Alyssa Johnston

I am an individual who thrives off change and challenging circumstances. From when I was very little, I wanted to push myself to be like my older siblings, learn new concepts, and challenge my mind and body. I love to learn and serve those around me because so much of my life has been formed by those who have loved and served me.

My roots run deep Family Always Comes First

I am the 4th out of 5 siblings. I have 2 sisters and 2 brothers. They are my best friends. I am grateful for their continued example of love, selflessness, and inclusion. My ideal weekend would be to spend time with them.

Around the World Traveling to Experience Life

After I graduated high school, I have created a fascination with traveling the world. Since 2016, I have visited countless countries and cities and have learned so much. I am grateful to experience cultures and lifestyles to love those around me more deeply.

Elder Holland Don't you Quit!

There are moments in my life where I can get discouraged, especially lately. However, I watch this video to help me re-center on what is truly important in life. God is my dear Father and He is here to support me and lift me in times where I know I can't do it alone. I will keep pushing forward because there is always a brighter future!
